Dr. Anver Amod

The history of the practice mirrors that of the Springfield community in Tallaght.
Dr. Ismail Coovadia founded it in Alderwood Springfield in 1975, coinciding with the community's establishment.
Upon his retirement in 2006, Dr. Anver Amod took over.
In 2016, the practice moved to the Primary Care Centre in the Russell building, Tallaght Cross West.
